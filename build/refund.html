Refund and Cancellation Policy

Last updated: May 1, 2026

Cancellations

You may cancel your account or subscription at any time. Cancellation requests will take effect immediately, preventing any future charges. However, cancellation does not retroactively refund previous payments.

Refunds

Due to the nature of our digital services, all sales are final. We do not offer refunds or credits for partially used billing periods or accidental purchases once a transaction has been successfully processed.

If a transaction fails but your account is debited, the amount will automatically be reversed to your original payment method by the payment gateway within 5-7 business days.
